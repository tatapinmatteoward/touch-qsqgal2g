## Why Diversify Your Stake?

Staking isn't just about locking up ETH and waiting for rewards. In a decentralized ecosystem like Ethereum, market fluctuations, protocol upgrades, and even slashing risks can impact your returns. Diversification spreads your exposure across multiple pools, validators, or even staking derivatives, ensuring that one underperformer doesn't drag down your entire portfolio.

With StakeWise, a leading liquid staking protocol, you can easily achieve this through its osETH token, which represents your staked ETH while remaining liquid. But how do you take it further? Let's break it down.

## Tactic 1: Mix Native and Liquid Staking

Don't put all your ETH in one basket. Combine **native staking** via the Ethereum Beacon Chain with **liquid staking** through StakeWise:

- **Native Staking**: Delegate to trusted validators for direct, high-yield rewards (around 4-5% APY currently).
- **Liquid Staking with osETH**: Stake via StakeWise to earn rewards while using osETH in DeFi for additional yields (e.g., lending on Aave or providing liquidity on Uniswap).

**Pro Tip**: Allocate 60% to native for stability and 40% to liquid for flexibility. This hybrid approach can boost overall APY by 1-2% through DeFi opportunities.

## Tactic 2: Validator Diversification

StakeWise aggregates validators from top providers like Lido, Rocket Pool, and independent operators. To minimize slashing risks:

- Spread across **geographically diverse validators** to avoid regional downtime.
- Choose a mix of **solo stakers** (decentralized) and **institutional validators** (reliable uptime).

Using StakeWise's dashboard, monitor validator performance and rebalance quarterly. Historical data shows diversified pools experience 20% less volatility in rewards.

## Tactic 3: Cross-Chain and Asset Diversification

Ethereum isn't the only game in town. Extend your StakeWise tactics to layer-2 solutions and other assets:

- Stake ETH on Optimism or Arbitrum via bridges, then use StakeWise-compatible wrappers.
- Diversify into restaking protocols like EigenLayer, where osETH can be restaked for extra rewards (up to 10% additional APY, but with higher risks).

**Risk Warning**: Restaking amplifies both rewards and potential lossesâ€”start small, no more than 20% of your portfolio.

Imagine staking 32 ETH: 16 ETH native (yielding ~5%), 10 ETH as osETH in DeFi (~7% combined), and 6 ETH restaked (~9%). Your blended APY? Around 6.5%, with reduced risk compared to all-in on one method.
