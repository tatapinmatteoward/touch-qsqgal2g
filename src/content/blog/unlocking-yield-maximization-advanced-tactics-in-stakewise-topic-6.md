## Tactical Breakdown: Step-by-Step Yield Strategies

### 1. **Layered Staking with osETH**
Start with the basics: stake your ETH via StakeWise to receive osETH. This token represents your staked position and can be used across DeFi. **Tactic**: Immediately allocate a portion to high-yield vaults. For instance, in a bull market, pair osETH with stablecoins on Curve for dual rewards—Ethereum staking yields plus trading fees.

*Pro Tip*: Monitor gas fees; StakeWise's entry/exit queues help during congestion, preserving your yields.

### 2. **Risk-Adjusted Allocation**
Not all yields are equal. Use StakeWise's analytics dashboard to assess impermanent loss and smart contract risks. **Tactic**: Diversify across 3-5 protocols. Allocate 40% to conservative lending (e.g., Aave), 30% to DEX liquidity, and 30% back to pure staking for stability.

This approach has historically delivered 8-12% annualized yields, outperforming solo staking by a wide margin.

### 3. **Automating for Efficiency**
Manual management is a yield killer. Integrate with automation tools like Gelato or Chainlink Keepers to rebalance your positions automatically. **Tactic**: Set triggers for when APYs drop below 7% to shift funds—StakeWise's API makes this seamless.
