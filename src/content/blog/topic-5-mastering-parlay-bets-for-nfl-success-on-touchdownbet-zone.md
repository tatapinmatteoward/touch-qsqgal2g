## What Are Parlay Bets?

A parlay bet combines multiple individual wagers into one. Instead of betting on a single game outcome, you link several—say, the winner of three NFL matchups. Win all legs, and you hit a massive multiplier on your stake. Lose just one, and the whole bet is bust. It's like threading a needle with your predictions, but the potential ROI? Sky-high.

For example, on TouchdownBet Zone, a simple two-leg parlay on Sunday's key games could multiply your $10 bet by 3x or more, depending on the odds.
